Research Interests

I am mostly interested in M-theory compactifications and special holonomy spaces, in particular those with G2-holonomy. These spaces are the natural candidates for models of the extra dimensions of spacetime that M-theory (and string theory) predicts and are also interesting mathematical objects in their own right. The geometry of these extra dimensions has a direct consequence on the physics observed in the remaining four-dimensional spacetime.

My Work

My work has centred largely on the physical interpretation of particular kinds of singularities inside G2-holonomy spaces. More recently, I have been interested in studying special holonomy orbifolds of Kummer-type (i.e. torus quotient orbifolds) and attempting to classify all of them in certain dimensions.
